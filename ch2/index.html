What Will I Learn in JCCC's Web Development and Digitial Media

Start a conversation or tell a story using the launage of technology. With just a a few semesters of study, you'll gain a deeper understanding of digitial workflow, web applications, web development and content management - and be ready to launch your new career in web development and digitial media.

Work in both Windows and Mac to build your tool box. Start with the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Move on to web scripting for front-end and back-end design and development using PHP, UX, UI, Bootstrap, jQuery, and React. Get experience building in WordPress and Drupal content management systems

In our Web Development and Digital Media courses, you'll learn:

  • Digital media concepts
  • Digital workflow
  • Web analytics
  • Search engine optimization (SEO)
  • User experience
  • Content and digital asset creation
  • Emerging technologies
  • Web scripting
